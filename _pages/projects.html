Research

My research interest is in exploring new approaches for turning design idea into reality. This includes automating the design process with Geometry Processing and exploring new fabrication methods with Additive Manufacturing.


Software Tool for Routing Efficiently Advanced Macrofluidics

  • Introduce a software based workflow that generates printable fluidic networks automatically.
  • Provide a library of fluidic components to implement any logic circuit.
